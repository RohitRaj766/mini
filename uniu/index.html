About Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, symbolizes the spiritual victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance. Homes are illuminated with diyas, rangoli decorates the floors, sweets are shared, and joy is celebrated with family and friends.
